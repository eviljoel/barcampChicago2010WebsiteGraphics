Agenda

This is the agenda for BARcamp Chicago 2010. Feel free to add your own events and workshops to the agenda.

You can pretty much talk about anything. But keep in mind, if your talk or workshop is more focused on marketing (rather than implementation details), then you will more than likely have a very small audience. Make sure to keep your talk or workshop interesting and fun!

Time blocks are not exclusive as we have plenty of space for multiple attendees to host talks/workshops at the same time. Also feel free to give a talk late Saturday night/Sunday morning.
